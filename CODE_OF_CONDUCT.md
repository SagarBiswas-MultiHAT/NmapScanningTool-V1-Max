# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not personal characteristics.
- Accept accountability and correct mistakes quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, discriminatory language, or hate speech.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that prevents productive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors for violations.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately to `conduct@multihat.dev`.
